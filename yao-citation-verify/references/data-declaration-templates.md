# 数据可用性声明模板

## CFPS（中国家庭追踪调查）

> The data used in this study are from the China Family Panel Studies (CFPS), conducted by the Institute of Social Science Survey (ISSS) of Peking University. The CFPS data are publicly available at https://opendata.pku.edu.cn/dataverse/CFPS. The data were accessed on [日期].

## CHFS（中国家庭金融调查）

> The data are from the China Household Finance Survey (CHFS), conducted by the Survey and Research Center for China Household Finance at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics. The CHFS data are available upon request at https://chfs.swufe.edu.cn.

## 中国统计年鉴

> Aggregate agricultural statistics are compiled from the China Statistical Yearbook and the China Rural Statistical Yearbook, published by the National Bureau of Statistics of China (https://www.stats.gov.cn).

## 县域统计年鉴

> County-level socioeconomic data are from the China County Statistical Yearbook, accessible through the China National Knowledge Infrastructure (CNKI) at https://www.cnki.net.

## 土地利用数据

> Land use/cover data are from the Resource and Environment Science and Data Center, Chinese Academy of Sciences (https://www.resdc.cn). The data have a spatial resolution of 30m × 30m.

## World Bank / FAO

> Agricultural production and food security indicators are from the World Bank World Development Indicators (https://databank.worldbank.org) and the Food and Agriculture Organization (https://www.fao.org/faostat).

## 混合数据声明

> This study uses multiple data sources. Household-level data are from [微观数据]. County-level socioeconomic data are from [宏观数据]. Land use data are from [土地利用数据]. Detailed variable definitions and summary statistics are provided in the supplementary materials.
